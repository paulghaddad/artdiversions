A little about Amy

Thank you for visiting my site!

John Lubbock Sr. once said: “Art is unquestionably one of the purest and highest elements in human happiness.” For me, there is much truth to this statement. Over two years ago, I created this blog to share my passion: writing about the arts.

For this blog, I write exhibition reviews and examine contemporary issues in art. I cover many art genres in my writing, but am most fascinated by artists using technology, such as virtual reality and creative coding. I also blog about technology at Tech Diversions.

I’ve contributed articles to Sculpture Magazine, Newcity, the Evanston Art Center and Create Hub. Previous publications include The Art House and NTQ-Data Limited.

Keep up with the changing art world on my site, and thanks for reading!
