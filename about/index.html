A little about Amy

Thank you for visiting my site!

John Lubbock Sr. once said: “Art is unquestionably one of the purest and highest elements in human happiness.” For me, there is much truth to this statement. I created this blog to share my passion: writing about the arts.

I am a freelance art writer and blogger, and love to write about the art world. For this blog, I write exhibition reviews, report on art fairs and biennials, conduct interviews with artists and examine contemporary issues in art. Although I have covered an array of topics in my writing, from ecological concerns to cultural icons, I am most fascinated by the impact of digital technology on art. In addition to this blog, I also write for Newcity in Chicago, and have contributed articles to the Columbus Museum of Art and NTQ-Data Limited.

The ever-changing art world is fascinating and exciting. I enjoy using the written word as an opportunity to analyze, think critically and share my passion with others.

Keep up with the changing art world and my journey on my site, and thanks for reading!
