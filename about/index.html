A little about Amy

“Writing is the painting of the voice.” -Voltaire

I am a writer at Veritas Health, where I contribute articles educating readers about sports injuries: symptoms, diagnosis, risk factors and treatment options.

Art is one of my passions in life, which is why I created this blog over two years ago. While my art writing covers many genres, I am most fascinated by artists using technology, such as virtual reality and creative coding. Given my interest in technology, I also blog about it at Tech Diversions. In addition to blogging, I contribute articles to Sculpture Magazine, Newcity and Create Hub.

Thank you for visiting my site!
