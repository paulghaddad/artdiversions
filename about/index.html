A little about Amy

Thank you for visiting my site!

John Lubbock Sr. once said: “Art is unquestionably one of the purest and highest elements in human happiness.” For me, there is much truth to this statement. I created this blog to share my passion: writing about the arts.

I am a freelance art writer and blogger. For this blog, I write exhibition reviews and examine contemporary issues in art. Although I cover a variety of genres in my writing, I am most fascinated by the impact of digital technology on art.

In addition to my blog, I am a freelance writer. I contribute articles to Sculpture Magazine, Newcity in Chicago, the Evanston Art Center and Create Hub. Previous publications include The Art House and NTQ-Data Limited.

Keep up with the changing art world on my site, and thanks for reading!
