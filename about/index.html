A little about Amy

“Writing is the painting of the voice.” -Voltaire

I am an editor at Veritas Health. I write articles educating readers about sports injuries: including how to recognize symptoms, reduce risk factors, get accurate diagnoses and understand treatment options.

Art is one of my passions in life, which is why I created this blog over two years ago. I write about all genres of art, but am most fascinated by artists using technology, such as virtual reality, robots and artificial intelligence. In addition to blogging, I contribute articles to several publications, including Sculpture Magazine, Newcity and Create Hub.

Thank you for visiting my site!
