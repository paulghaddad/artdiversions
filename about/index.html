A little about Amy

Thank you for visiting my site!

John Lubbock Sr. once said: “Art is unquestionably one of the purest and highest elements in human happiness.” For me, there is much truth to this statement. Over two years ago, I created this blog to share my passion: writing about the arts.

For this blog, I write exhibition reviews and examine contemporary issues in art. Although I cover many art genres in my writing, I am most fascinated by the impact of technology on art.

Outside this blog, I’ve contributed articles to Sculpture Magazine, Newcity, the Evanston Art Center and Create Hub. Previous publications include The Art House and NTQ-Data Limited.

Keep up with the changing art world and my journey on my site, and thanks for reading!
